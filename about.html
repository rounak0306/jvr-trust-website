Who We Are

     

        JVR Trust is dedicated to transforming lives through inclusive, high-quality education.

        Founded to expand access in underserved regions, the trust establishes and manages

        professional colleges and vocational centres that combine practical training, industry

        partnerships and community outreach to build career pathways for students.
